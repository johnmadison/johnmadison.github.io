PERMIT FEE APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS

New Construction:
To determine the applicable building permit fee for new construction, including additions, refer to the Building Valuation Data Sheet. Locate the use of the building from the left column. Locate the construction type from one of the vertical columns. For residential one and two family construction and their related structures, use the VB (far right) column for square foot construction costs.

Determine the total valuation of the project by muliplying the area in square feet of the structure by the dollar amount indicated on the table.

Refer to the Table 108-A Mesa County Building Permit Fee Schedule and locate the valuation from the Data chart for the correct permit fee. Valuations will be rounded up in all cases.

Remodel Work:
Determine the applicable building permit fee for remodel work and all other building up-grades from Table 108-A Mesa County Building Permit Fee Schedule, based on the valutation of the project.

Miscellaneous fees:
Refer to Other Inspection and Fees schedule for all other permit fees.
